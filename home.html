Get to know the world

Africa

Vibrant, rhythmic, and rooted in tradition.

Africa is home to rich percussive sounds, Afrobeat, Amapiano, Highlife, and countless local styles that blend storytelling with movement. Countries like Nigeria, South Africa, and Mali lead musical innovation globally.

Asia

Diverse, dynamic, and deeply rooted in culture.

From the hypnotic beats of Arabic pop and classical maqam to the global waves of K-pop, Bollywood, and J-pop, Asia's music tells countless stories. Whether it's the soulful strings of the oud or the basslines of underground scenes, every region adds its unique voice to the continent’s vibrant sound.

Europe

A mix of classical heritage and electronic revolution.

Europe balances its classical roots with chart-topping pop, techno from Berlin, French rap, UK drill, and Eurovision-fueled experimentation.

North America

The birthplace of many modern genres.

From hip-hop, jazz, country, and R&B to Latin crossover hits in Mexico and the Caribbean, North America blends deep roots with cultural reinvention.

South America

Passionate, rhythmic, and rich with dance.

Whether it's Brazil's samba and funk, Argentina’s tango, or Colombia’s reggaeton and cumbia, South American music is inseparable from movement and spirit.

Oceania

Laid-back, soulful, and rooted in nature.

Australia and New Zealand bring indie, electronic, and surf-inspired sounds — often intertwined with Indigenous storytelling and Polynesian harmonies.
